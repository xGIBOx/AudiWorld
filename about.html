READ ABOUT US

AudiWorld specializes in exclusive automotive experiences, offering exceptional service tailored to our clients. Our mission revolves around creating automotive spaces that mirror our clients' unique design preferences and lifestyle. We are dedicated to curating superior automotive collections, enhancing functionality, and elevating the value of our clients' vehicles. Whether collaborating with enthusiasts across various models, we bring a versatile aesthetic to our designs, seamlessly executing contemporary, classic, and innovative automotive concepts. Our success is attributed to an extensive inventory, boasting over twenty thousand curated pieces.

In terms of customization, we have successfully completed comprehensive automotive installations, personalized features, and bespoke enhancements in as little as three and a half weeks.

WHERE IT STARTED

AudiWorld was established in 2010 by Bogdan 'Gibo' Živković. Bogdan has a wealth of experience in the automotive industry, having spent numerous years working with renowned automotive companies and acquiring valuable insights.

Every vehicle we curate is the result of a dynamic partnership between our client's preferences and our automotive design expertise. As a reputable dealer endorsed by premier brands, we deliver exceptional service in car sales, vehicle tuning, and maintenance. Our commitment is to provide our clients with an unparalleled experience, ensuring that each aspect of their automotive journey is meticulously crafted to perfection.
